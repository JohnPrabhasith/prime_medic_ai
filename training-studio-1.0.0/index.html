What is Prime Medic AI?

Prime Medic AI is an AI-powered virtual health assistant that provides fast, accessible, and accurate medical guidance for minor health concerns. Whether you’re feeling unwell, unsure about symptoms, or need help deciding if you should visit a doctor — we’re here to guide you.
